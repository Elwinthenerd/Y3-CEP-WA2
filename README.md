# Y3-CEP-WA2
My Year 3 Computer Elective Programme WA2 repository.

This project simulates a small field-based world with:
•	moving red and blue bodies
•	inverse-square attraction and repulsion
•	static accelerator zones
•	trails
•	heatmap and vector-field overlays
•	seeded randomness
•	a scrollable science explanation panel

Controls

•	S cycle spawn type
•	X hide or show the right menu
•	H toggle heatmap
•	V toggle vector field
•	T cycle trails
•	Space pause or unpause
•	, slow motion
•	. step one frame while paused
•	> fast forward
•	R restart same seed
•	N fresh seed
•	O reset camera
•	Esc clear all particles and accelerator zones
•	! open or close science explanation

Mouse:
•	Click empty space to spawn
•	Drag from a body to launch it
•	Mouse wheel zooms the sim, or scrolls the science panel when it is open
